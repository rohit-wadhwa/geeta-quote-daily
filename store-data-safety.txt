# Data Safety for Geeta Quote Daily Extension

## How we handle your data

Geeta Quote Daily respects your privacy completely. Our extension:

- Does not collect any personal data
- Stores preferences only on your device
- Never transmits any user data to external servers
- Uses permissions only for essential functionality

## Permissions we request

- **Storage**: Saves your preferences locally (like language choice and sound settings)
- **Alarms**: Schedules daily quote refreshes at midnight
- **Host permissions**: Accesses only specific domains to fetch quotes, music, and sounds

All data stays on your device, and we have no access to it. The extension works fully offline once content is cached.
